## Overview

To use Autoch.at, sign up and create your first project.

## Steps

1. Go to `https://autoch.at/signup`
2. Register for a free account
3. Log in to your dashboard
4. Click "New Project"
5. Name your project and save

You can now organize and manage documentation or bots for your project.
