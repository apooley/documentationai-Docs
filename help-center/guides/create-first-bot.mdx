## Overview

Build a basic bot to automate replies in minutes.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new bot">
    Click "New Bot" on your project dashboard.
    Name your bot (e.g., SupportBot).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add responses">
    Define triggers and replies (e.g., when someone says "hello", reply "Hi, how can I help?").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test your bot">
    Click "Test" to simulate a chat session and check responses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Press "Activate" to start using your bot.
  </Step>
</Steps>
