---
title: Cannot connect to dashboard
description: What to do if you can't access your Autoch.at dashboard.
---

## Symptoms

- Dashboard won't load
- Error: "Unable to connect" or timeout

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    ````bash
    ping autoch.at
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try incognito window">
    Open Autoch.at using private browsing to avoid cache issues.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    Email support@autoch.at if the issue persists.
  </Step>
</Steps>
